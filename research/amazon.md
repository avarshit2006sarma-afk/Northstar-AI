# Amazon Internship Research

## Overview

Amazon hires engineers who demonstrate strong ownership, problem-solving ability, technical competence, and customer-focused thinking. The hiring process heavily incorporates Amazon Leadership Principles alongside technical skills.

---

# Core Skills Valued

* Data Structures and Algorithms
* Object-Oriented Programming
* Problem Solving
* Software Engineering Principles
* System Design Basics
* Debugging
* Scalability Awareness

---

# Importance of Projects

Projects should demonstrate:

* Ownership
* Real-world impact
* Scalability
* Problem solving
* Technical depth

Amazon values builders who can take an idea from concept to deployment.

---

# Importance of DSA

Very High.

Common interview topics include:

* Arrays
* Strings
* Trees
* Graphs
* Dynamic Programming
* Sliding Window
* BFS / DFS
* Hash Maps

Efficiency and optimization are important.

---

# Internship Experience

Internships significantly strengthen a profile.

Ownership and measurable impact during internships are more valuable than company brand alone.

---

# Resume Expectations

Preferred resumes:

* One page
* Action verbs
* Quantified impact
* Customer-focused achievements
* Strong project descriptions

---

# Leadership Principles

Amazon strongly evaluates alignment with its Leadership Principles, including:

* Ownership
* Customer Obsession
* Learn and Be Curious
* Bias for Action
* Deliver Results
* Invent and Simplify

Behavioral interviews heavily focus on these principles.

---

# GitHub

Recruiters appreciate:

* Active repositories
* Meaningful projects
* Consistent commits
* Clear documentation

---

# Soft Skills

Amazon values:

* Ownership
* Accountability
* Communication
* Teamwork
* Adaptability

---

# Interview Process

Typical stages:

1. Resume Screening
2. Online Assessment
3. Technical Interview
4. Leadership Principle Interview

Behavioral performance is often as important as technical performance.

---

# Repeated Recruiter Advice

* Practice DSA consistently.
* Build projects with measurable impact.
* Prepare STAR-format behavioral answers.
* Demonstrate ownership.
* Focus on solving customer problems.
